India

My trip to India was with my freshman year roommate, Vay, who let me join in on a family vacation! In India, we went to 3 main areas: Mumbai, Goa and New Delhi.

In Mumbai, we were able to visit Vay's extended family and also explore the city.

The main purpose of our trip to New Delhi was to see the Taj Mahal.

In Goa, we enjoyed the warm tropical weather and had the opportunity to relax after all of the site-seeing.
